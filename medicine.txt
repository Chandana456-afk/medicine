Requirements for medical school vary by country and program. They can include: 
Education: A high school diploma or equivalent, and a bachelor's degree in a relevant subject 
Entrance exams: Passing a medical entrance exam, such as the NEET in India or the UCAT in the UK 
Grades: Maintaining a minimum grade point average 
Subjects: Taking courses in physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics 
Work experience: Having relevant work experience 
English language: Meeting English language requirements, such as an IELTS or TOEFL score 
Personal statement: Writing a personal statement that meets the program's criteria 
Interview: Participating in an interview process
